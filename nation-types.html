Nation Types

Learn about the different types of nations in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Nation Types Overview

  • Player Nation: Regular nations controlled by players.
  • Mod Nation: Nations played by moderators with extra restrictions and rewards for their work. Mod nations:
    • May see the full map and expand without exploring, unless in a frontier.
    • Cannot post exploration roleplay.
    • Receive 100 coins per month as payment for mod work.
    • Cannot declare war against a single player nation unless that nation is dominant in their region.
    • Cannot meta-game or share internal information about undiscovered parts of the game.
  • NPC: Non-player nations controlled by moderators. They have limited stats (lifestyle and one culture ability) and are used for narrative purposes.
