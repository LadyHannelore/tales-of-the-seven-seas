Nation Types

Player Nation

Regular nations by players.

  • Standard gameplay experience
  • Full access to all mechanics
  • Can engage in exploration, warfare, diplomacy, and trade
  • Must follow standard coin generation and resource rules

Mod Nation

Nations played by mods. They have extra restrictions and rewards from their mod work.

Special Abilities

  • Mod nation may see the full map, and expand without exploring, unless in frontier
  • Mod nations cannot post exploration roleplay
  • Mod nation get 100 coins per month as payment for mod work

Restrictions

  • Two Mod nations cannot declare war against one player nation, unless that player nation is dominant in their region
  • Mod nation cannot meta-game and share internal information relating to undiscovered parts of the game

NPC

NPC nations played by mods. The only two stats they have are lifestyle and 1 culture ability. They are used for narrative purposes as deemed appropriate.

  • Controlled entirely by moderators
  • Limited stats for streamlined gameplay
  • Serve story and worldbuilding purposes
  • Cannot be played by regular players
