Spawning

Create your fictional nation and embark on an epic journey in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Spawning Overview

You must create a fictional nation to play as. You can't play as any real culture or people, but can be inspired by them, putting a spin on it. Your worldbuilding can be as creative as you want, but no sci-fi or fantasy.

  1. Select a real-life region from the region map shown below, which corresponds to an alternate location in the gameâ€™s alternate earth map.
  2. React to get a role for that region. You can only pick one region.
  3. Create a spawning ticket in the designated channel.
  4. List the following details:
    • Username
    • Nation name
    • Lifestyle
    • Capital (fictional)
    • A map showing your capital, 1 more settlement, and 5 tiles (including your settlements), which must be contiguous by land, or no further than a neighboring sea tile by sea.
  5. The spawning mod will confirm your spawn and send you a map showing your final spawn. You may change your capital or lifestyle to fit your map location and can select a new tile if your selected tile isn't available on the map.
