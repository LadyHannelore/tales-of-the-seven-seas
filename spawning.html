Spawning

You must create a fictional nation to play as. You can't play as any real culture or people, but can be inspired by them, putting a spin on it. Your worldbuilding can be as creative as you want, but no sci-fi or fantasy.

Spawning Process

  1. Select a real life region from the region map shown below, which corresponds to an alternate location in the game's alternate earth map.
  2. React to get a role for that region in (). You can only pick one region.
  3. Create a spawning ticket in ()
  4. List the following details:
    • Username
    • Nation name
    • Lifestyle
    • Capital (fictional)
    • A map showing your capital, 1 more settlement, and 5 tiles (including your settlements), which must be contiguous by land, or no further than a neighboring sea tile by sea.
  5. Confirmation: The spawning mod will confirm your spawn, and send you a map showing your final spawn. You may change your capital or lifestyle to fit your map location, and can select a new tile if your selected tile isn't available on the map.

Lifestyle Options

You must choose 1 lifestyle at spawn, which will give you specific bonuses:

Farmer

Settled crops and livestock farmers of the world.

  • Produce 1 food per tile (× tile modifier)
  • Start off with 1 crop of your design
  • Can expand into herder and gatherer land without declaring war

Gatherer

Hunter, forager, and fishermen of the world.

  • Produce ½ food per tile (× tile modifier)
  • May select 1 native terrain (excluding open sea/impassable terrain), and gain 1 food per tile in that terrain (1.5 food per tile total)
  • Will produce food from every tile type, regardless of domestic lifeforms
  • When exploring, if you discover a plant or animal, gain 3 food. If hostile, must roleplay to defeat it

Herder

Nomadic pastoralists of the world.

  • Produce ⅓ food per tile (× tile modifier)
  • Start off with 1 livestock of your design
  • Expansion cost is 5 per grassland and desert tile (¼ normal cost)
  • Each settlement has 10 tile expansion range and 10 tile trade range
  • May convert each food to 1 mount
