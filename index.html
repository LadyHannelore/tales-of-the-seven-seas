Welcome to a World Reborn

In the aftermath of a great cataclysm, civilization must begin anew. You are the architect of a nascent nation in an age of bronze and discovery. Will you lead farmers to till fertile valleys? Guide nomadic herders across vast steppes? Or forge coastal settlements to master the seas?

Tales of the Seven Seas is an exploration-worldbuilding nation roleplay game where you shape history itself. Starting in the Bronze Age on an alternate Earth, every decision carves your legacy into the annals of time.

Quick Reference Guide

🌾 Lifestyle Paths

  • Farmer: Settled agriculture
    1 food per farmland tile
  • Gatherer: Hunting & foraging
    ½ food per tile, works on all terrain
  • Herder: Nomadic pastoralism
    ⅓ food per tile, reduced expansion costs

💰 Core Resources

  • Food: Sustains population, enables expansion
  • Labor: Population workforce for production
  • Coins: Primary currency from worldbuilding
  • Timber: Construction material from forests
  • Bronze: Advanced metal for tools and weapons

🏘️ Settlement Hierarchy

  • Town: 1 population
    Cost: 2 food, 1 labor
  • City: 3 population + income
    Cost: 2 copper, 1 bronze, 3 timber, 25 coins
  • Metropolis: 5 population + enhanced benefits
    Cost: 4 bronze, 50 coins, 3 unique resources
