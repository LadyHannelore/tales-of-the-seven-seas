Welcome to Tales of the Seven Seas!

Tales of the 7 seas is an exploration-worldbuilding based nation roleplay game, where you build fictional cultures on an alternate-geography map. Civilization started over and YOU get to build it, starting at the bronze age. Are you ready for the challenge?

Quick Reference

Lifestyles

  • Farmer: Settled agriculture (1 food per tile)
  • Gatherer: Hunting & foraging (½ food per tile, all terrain)
  • Herder: Nomadic pastoralism (⅓ food per tile, cheaper expansion)

Resources

  • Food: Feeds population, exploration, construction
  • Timber: Construction material from forests
  • Bronze: Made from copper + tin, used in warfare
  • Coins: Main currency from worldbuilding
