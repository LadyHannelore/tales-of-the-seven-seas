Welcome to Tales of the Seven Seas!

Tales of the Seven Seas is an exploration-worldbuilding based nation roleplay game, where you build fictional cultures on an alternate-geography map. Civilization started over and YOU get to build it, starting at the Bronze Age. Are you ready for a challenge?
