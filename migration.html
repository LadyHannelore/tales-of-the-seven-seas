Migration

Learn about emergency relocation and strategies in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Migration Overview

In an emergency situation, like when you're being fully annexed, or if you really wish to leave your area, you may choose to migrate your nation, which acts as a soft respawn. This can only be done once in your entire gameplay.

Steps to migrate:

  1. Ping the spawning mod who will remove your region role.
  2. Select a new region role where you choose to migrate to, preferably a known region, and spawn using the spawning ticket.

You may spawn with:

  • Your culture abilities, lifestyle, and technology (or choose to lose them).
  • Up to 20% of your population (no more than 10).
  • 1 tile per population to spawn in.
  • Settlements for your population made from your leftover resources and coins. If you cannot build settlements for some of your population, you cannot spawn with that population.

Any leftover resources, population, settlements, and coins will either disappear or go to anyone who conquers your land.
