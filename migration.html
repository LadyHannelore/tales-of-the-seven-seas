Migration

Learn about emergency relocation and strategies in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Migration Overview

In an emergency, you may choose to migrate your nation—acting as a soft respawn—once per gameplay.

Steps to migrate:

  1. Ping the spawning mod who will remove your region role.
  2. Select a new region role and spawn using the spawning ticket.

You may spawn with:

  • Your culture abilities, lifestyle, and technology (or choose to reset).
  • Up to 20% of your population (max 10).
  • One tile per population.
  • Settlements built from leftover resources and coins.

Any leftover resources, population, settlements, and coins will disappear or go to your conqueror.
