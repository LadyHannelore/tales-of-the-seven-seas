Migration

In an emergency situation, like when you're being fully annexed, or if you really wish to leave your area, you may choose to migrate your nation, which acts as a soft respawn. This can only be done once in your entire gameplay.

Migration Process

  1. You must ping the spawning mod who will remove your region role
  2. Then, select a new region role where you choose to migrate to, preferably a known region
  3. Spawn using the spawning ticket

What You Can Bring

You may spawn with:

  • Culture & Technology: Your culture abilities, lifestyle and technology (or choose to lose them)
  • Population: Up to 20% of your population (no more than 10)
  • Territory: 1 tile per population to spawn in
  • Settlements: Settlements for your population made from your leftover resources and coins. If you cannot build settlements for some of your population, you cannot spawn with that population

What You Lose

Any leftover resources, population, settlements and coins will either disappear, or go to anyone who conquers your land.

⚠️ Important Restrictions

  • Migration can only be used once per entire gameplay
  • You must be able to build settlements for all migrating population
  • Migration is intended for emergency situations only
  • Consider diplomacy and treaties before choosing migration

Strategic Considerations

When to Consider Migration

  • Your nation is being completely annexed
  • You're surrounded by hostile nations with no diplomatic options
  • Your current region no longer offers growth opportunities
  • You want to start fresh in a different part of the world

Migration Planning

  • Calculate which population and resources you can afford to bring
  • Research potential destination regions before migrating
  • Consider keeping beneficial technologies and culture abilities
  • Plan your new spawn location carefully for future expansion
