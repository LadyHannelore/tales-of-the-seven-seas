Coins

Coins are the main currency of the game, earned through worldbuilding and roleplay. Use coins for expansion, exploration, construction, and trade.

Worldbuilding

Enrich your nation with lore. Worldbuilding posts generate coins (graded 0-30 based on quality). You may post roleplay, art, essays, short stories, memes, descriptions, or other works.

  • Posts can be 100% fictional or mix real/fictitious inspiration.
  • Collaboration allowed; coins split between contributors.
  • No AI-generated content.
  • Roleplay & Short Story: ≥50 words. Post in worldbuilding chat; ungraded roleplay can go in Town Square.
  • Art: Any nation-themed artwork; hand-drawn maps count.
  • Essay & Description: ≥50 words detailing aspects and inspirations.
  • Memes: Graded lower than other types.

Hall of Fame

Exceptional worldbuilding may be canonized. If you allow, each selected post grants +5 coins and is used in future game lore with your credit.

⭐ Posts awarded by mods earn 40–100 coins and feature in game promotions with credit.
