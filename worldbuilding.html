Worldbuilding Overview

Worldbuilding posts drive storytelling and coin generation. Share roleplay, art, essays, short stories, memes, or descriptions—all creative works that enrich your nation’s lore!

Posts must be 100% fictional—research for inspiration is allowed, but keep your descriptions in-character.

AI-generated content is not permitted.

  • Roleplay & Short Story: At least 50 words of character-driven narrative.
  • Art: Illustrations of lifeforms, culture, events, and more.
  • Essay & Description: Detailed essays or descriptions, 50+ words.
  • Memes: Humorous or satirical posts—graded modestly.

Collaboration divides award coins, and exceptional posts may earn special rewards like canonization or Hall of Fame induction.
