Basic Mechanics

Learn the core gameplay mechanics to thrive in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Gameplay Overview

After selecting the region, your gameplay will mostly be contained within your regional chat, which will include:

  • Map & Sheet: Basic information about every nation, updated each cycle.
  • Worldbuilding: Roleplay, art, essays, or music to generate coins.
  • Expansion: Grow your nation and food production with coins.
  • Resources: Automate food and produce resources via labor or worldbuilding.
  • Settlements: Build nodes of defense, trade, and labor generation.
  • Trade: Exchange goods with nearby nations.
  • Technology: Research, breed new lifeforms, or trade advancements.

Some mechanics occur via tickets:

  • Culture: Unlock culture bonuses.
  • Exploration: Discover and interact with distant lands for coins.
  • War: Engage in conflict for land and resources.
