Basic Mechanics

Learn the core gameplay mechanics to thrive in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Gameplay Overview

After selecting the region, your gameplay will mostly be contained within your regional chat, which will include:

  • Map & Sheet: Basic information about every nation in the region will be contained in the region’s sheet. Every cycle, the map will also be updated within this chat.
  • Worldbuilding: Post worldbuilding, roleplay, art, essays, memes, music, or other posts relating to your region to generate coins.
  • Expansion: Grow your nation with coins and increase your food production.
  • Resources: Food will be automatically generated from all your land, while other resources can be produced from your land using labor or worldbuilding.
  • Settlements: Use resources to build settlements, which will generate labor, defense, and act as nodes of trade.
  • Trade: Trade anything with other nations in your region or surrounding regions.
  • Technology: Research new technologies, breed new life forms, or trade your technology.

Additionally, some mechanics are not contained within your regional chat and will be done through specific tickets:

  • Culture: Fulfill requirements and unlock culture bonuses.
  • Exploration: Discover, interact, and trade with faraway lands while roleplaying for coins along the way.
  • War: Fight surrounding nations for land, resources, and dominance.
