Basic Mechanics

Master the fundamental systems that drive your nation's growth and prosperity

After selecting the region, your gameplay will mostly be contained within your regional chat, which will be a forum containing the following chat:

Regional Chat Channels

Map & Sheet

Basic information about every nation in the region will be contained in the region's sheet. Every cycle, the map will also be updated within this chat.

Worldbuilding

Posting worldbuilding, roleplay, art, essays, memes, music, or other posts relating to your region to generate coins.

Expansion

Allows you to grow your nation with coins and increase your food production.

Resources

Food will be automatically generated from all your land, while other resources can be produced from your land using labor or Worldbuilding.

Settlements

You may use the resource to build settlements, which will generate labor, defense and act as a node of trade.

Trade

You may trade anything with other nations in your region or surrounding regions.

Technology

Where you research new technologies, breed new life forms, or trade your technology.

Additional Mechanics

Additionally, there are some mechanics not contained within your regional chat. These will be done through specific tickets found in (link):

  • Culture: Where you fulfill requirements and unlock culture bonus
  • Exploration: Where you discover, interact and trade with faraway land, while roleplaying for coins along the way
  • War: Where you fight surrounding nations for land, resources, and dominance

Core Resource System

Population & Labor

Your nation will have a certain population calculated from your settlements. Each population will consume 1 food, and produce 1 labor. You may use this labor to produce resources or fight wars.

Food Production

Food automatically spawns from tiles you have domestic lifeforms in (gatherers generate food from every tile). Will regenerate each cycle (14 days):

food per tile = lifestyle modifier × terrain modifier Total food = [farmland + (domestic lifeforms × 2) + native terrain food(gatherer) - population] rounded

Settlement Types

  • Town: 2 food, 1 labor → 1 population
  • City: 2 copper, 1 bronze, 3 timber, 25 coins, 1 unique resource → 3 population + 15 coins passive income with law tech
  • Metropolis: 4 bronze, 50 coins, 3 unique resources → 5 population + 30 coins passive income with law tech + can produce books
