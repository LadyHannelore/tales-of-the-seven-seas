War

Master strategies and lead your nation to victory in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Revised War System

Casus Belli

  • Skirmish:
    • Declaration: Declare 1-20 tiles to capture.
    • Victory Requirement: Hold >50% of declared tiles by end of Turn 4 OR opponent surrenders.
    • Victory: Capture declared tiles + 1 loot per 3 tiles held.
    • Defeat: Opponent may demand 50% of undeclared tiles they hold.
  • Conquest:
    • Declaration: Declare 1-3 settlements to capture (no capitals unless only city).
    • Victory Requirement: Capture settlements within 6 turns.
    • Victory: Gain settlements + 3 tiles/settlement (must maintain territorial contiguity).
    • Defeat: Lose settlements + opponent may relocate orphaned tiles.
  • Raid:
    • Declaration: Declare loot target (1-15).
    • Victory Requirement: Generate loot through battles/sieges within 4 turns.
    • Victory: Gain declared loot.
    • Defeat: Pay opponent 1/2 declared loot value.
  • Subjugation:
    • Declaration: State conditions (vassalization, cultural change, etc.).
    • Victory Requirement: Capture capital OR hold 50% settlements for 2 turns.
    • Victory: Enforce conditions + 1 settlement/3 tiles/2 loot.
    • Defeat: Opponent may demand 1 settlement OR 3 tiles OR 2 loot.

Army System

  • Creation Cost: 1 Labor (halts regeneration only during active battle turns).
  • Maintenance: After Turn 3, costs 1 Food/army/turn.
  • Settlement Limits:
    • Town: 1 army.
    • City: 3 armies.
    • Metropolis: 5 armies.

New Mechanics

  • Supply Lines: Armies beyond expansion range 2+ turns lose 1 random unit/turn.
  • War Weariness:
    • After Turn 4: +1 Food maintenance/army/turn.
    • After Turn 6: -1 Combat Strength to all units.
  • Diplomatic Penalties: Aggressor loses 1 Diplomatic Favor per war declaration.
  • Scouting: 1 free Scout unit per army:
    • Movement: 5 tiles.
    • Reveals enemy positions in adjacent tiles.
    • Cannot attack/be attacked.
