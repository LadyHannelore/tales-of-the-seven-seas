Map & Visibility

Learn about the game map and visibility mechanics in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Map Overview

Tales of the Seven Seas uses an alternate earth map, where each real-world location corresponds to an alternate location in the game. Your nation’s lore isn’t bound by actual history—feel free to explore new terrains!

Every 14 days on Monday, an updated map posts in your region’s chat, showing hexagonal tiles for land, settlements, and resources. Use the latest map for expansions or construction; you must own the tile to interact with it.

DO NOT share your region map with other players or in general chat—you’ll spoil the exploration aspect. Feel free to share and annotate hand-drawn versions.

Visibility

Interact only with tiles visible on your region’s map. Each settlement grants a 5-tile visibility radius by land and sea for the entire region, allowing expansions and trade within that zone.

You can also see up to 3 tiles beyond your region (marked by a lighter overlay), enabling trade or expansion into neighboring areas. Sea and impassable terrain block further visibility.
