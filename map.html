The Alternate Earth Map

Tales of the Seven Seas uses an alternate-earth map where each real-world location corresponds to an alternate location. Your nation’s history is unique—terrain may differ. Experiment with new areas!

Map Updates

Every 14 days (Monday), an updated map is posted in your region’s Map & Sheet channel. The hex grid shows land tiles, settlements, and resource spawns. Always use the latest map for expansions and construction. You must own territory to interact with it.

Privacy & Sharing

Do not share your region map outside your region or in general chat—this preserves exploration for all. Hand-drawn maps are allowed and may be altered freely.

Visibility Rules

  • Each settlement grants a 5-tile visibility range by land and sea.
  • Visible tiles allow expansion or trade within the settlement’s range.
  • You can see up to 3 tiles beyond your region (lighter overlay). These mark neighboring regions. Sea barriers block visibility.
  • Impassable terrain (e.g. high mountains, sea ice) also blocks line of sight.
