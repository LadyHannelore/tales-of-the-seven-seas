Terrain

Learn about terrain types and their effects in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Terrain Types

Each tile has a different terrain with its own effects. They are separated into four categories: Regular, Harsh, Special, and Sea.

  • Regular Terrain: x1 terrain modifier for food production.
    • Tropical Dry Forest
    • Subtropical Forest
    • Mediterranean Forest
    • Oceanic Forest
    • Continental Forest
    • Montane Broadleaf Forest
    • Savanna Grassland
    • Prairie Grassland
  • Harsh Terrain: x0.5 terrain modifier for food production.
    • Tropical Rainforest
    • Boreal Forest
    • Montane Coniferous Forest
    • Tundra Grassland
    • Hot Desert
    • Cold Desert
  • Special Terrain:
    • Riverine: x3 terrain modifier for food production. Does not require native crops/livestock to produce food.
    • Ice & High Mountains: Impassable. Cannot be expanded into.
  • Sea Terrain: Cannot be owned, but can be expanded through.
    • Calm Sea: Sailable after unlocking Shipbuilding.
    • Open Sea: Sailable after unlocking Open Sea Sailing.
    • Rough Sea, Quiet Sea, Arctic Sea, Sea Ice: Impassable, cannot be expanded through.
