Exploration Overview

Ready to send your explorers across the seven seas? Start an exploration campaign to discover new lands, roleplaying your journey along the way! Meet new regions and nations to trade with, befriend, or fight. Explore as far as your skill will lead you.

Create a ticket in the designated channel to begin. You may invite 1-2 allied nations to join, starting along your border.

Each campaign is done in turns with three stages:

  • Plan: Select 5 tiles to explore and pay 1 food, 1 mount, or 30 coins.
  • Dice Roll: Roll a 1d10 to determine outcomes—discoveries, challenges, or disasters.
  • Roleplay: Narrate your journey, especially during challenges; roleplay earns extra rewards.

After each turn, the exploration mod posts an updated journey map. If you hit impassable terrain, your next turn resumes from the last passable tile.
