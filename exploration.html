Territory

Each playerâ€™s territory is marked by their color on map tiles. You may only build settlements, worldbuild, or spawn resources within your territory.

Expansions

Grow your territory at 20 coins per tile (herders cost less). Tiles must be unoccupied, within settlement range (land 3, calm sea 3, open sea 5), visible on the map (including 3 tiles beyond border), non-impassable, and contiguous. Expansions window runs from map post until the following Friday.

Population & Labor

Population comes from settlements. Each person consumes 1 food and produces 1 labor per cycle. Labor powers resource spawning and armies.

Resources

  • Food: Auto-generated per tile each cycle.
  • Timber: Spawnable from forest tiles.
  • Copper: Spawnable from copper tiles.
  • Tin: Spawnable from tin tiles.
  • Mount: Spawnable from native habitat tiles.
  • Books: Produced in metropolises.

Settlement Types

  • Town: Tier 1, costs 3 food + 1 labor, 1 population, range 3.
  • City: Tier 2, upgrade costs 1 copper + 3 timber + 25 silver + 2 unique resources, 3 population, range 3.
  • Metropolis: Tier 3, upgrade costs 2 bronze + 3 timber + 100 silver + 5 unique resources, 5 population, range 3, produces Books.

Trade & Lifestyle

Trade goods within your settlement range. Choose one lifestyle at spawn for bonuses:

  • Farmer: +1 food/tile; starts with one domesticated plant.
  • Gatherer: +0.5 food/tile; pick one terrain for +1 food/tile (max 30 tiles).
  • Herder: +0.33 food/tile; starts with one domesticated animal; cheaper expansion.
