Exploration

Discover uncharted territories and expand your nation's horizons in Tales of the Seven Seas.

Exploration Overview

Ready to send your explorers across the seven seas? Start an exploration campaign to discover new lands, roleplaying your journey along the way! You will meet new regions and nations to trade with, befriend, or fight. Explore as far as your skill will lead you!

Each player may create a ticket in the designated channel to begin their journey. They may also invite 1-2 nations within any region they have land in to join them at the start. The explorer will start anywhere along the border of the original nation who started the campaign.

Each exploration campaign will be done in turns, divided into three stages: Plan, Dice Roll, and Roleplay.

  • Plan: One player must select 5 tiles to explore and pay 1 food, 1 mount, or 30 coins.
  • Dice Roll: Roll a 1d10 dice to determine what happens to your explorer. Outcomes include discoveries, challenges, or even disasters.
  • Roleplay: Roleplay your journey, especially during challenges. Your roleplay will be graded at a higher rate than usual.

After each turn, the exploration mod will post an updated map of the journey. The explorer will continue from the last tile they ended up in. If an explorer hits an impassable tile, their next turn will begin from the last passable tile they passed through.
